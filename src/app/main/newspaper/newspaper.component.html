Talking About Newspaper

News Paper is a large outdoor advertising structure that presents large advertisements to passing pedestrians and drivers. It is typically found in high-traffic areas such as alongside busy roads. This is most useful way to advertising a company

Classified advertisements

There are no spectacular titles in the classified ads. The price of such advertisements is a total of BDT 1450 for the first 15 words and BDT 60 for every subsequent word. An additional 15% VAT will be added to the advertising price. Classified ads must be in a maximum of 40 words.
